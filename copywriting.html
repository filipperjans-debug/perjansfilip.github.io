Agency of the Year 2024

Nominated. Agency of the Year.

Bang the drumroll! We are thrilled to announce that we’ve been nominated for Agency of the Year 2024. The nod comes straight from Regi's annual industry and client survey, now in its 24th edition. Once again, proof that we’re still rocking the communication game …

The winners will be unveiled at the Regi annual gala on February 22 at Sergel Hub in Stockholm. To make the cut of being nominated, agencies need both a hefty clientele and top-tier satisfaction across 15 criteria, which makes us even more proud of being nominated.

“We are so proud to be among Sweden's top-ranked agencies. After only 4 years as an agency group, we have a turnover of almost 100 million and are among the agencies that receive the absolute highest ratings from their clients!” – Patrick Walldén, founder and CEO, BANG Agency

We at BANG are all in for the gala festivities, gearing up for an evening of celebration and recognition. We're pumped for the opportunity to continue delivering banging communication for our clients. And yet again, a big thanks to our great customers for your trust!
