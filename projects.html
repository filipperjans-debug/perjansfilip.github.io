Reuse like
there’s a tomorrow.
Rekoroben, an exclusive second-hand store in Karlstad, aimed to increase its brand awareness and reach a wider customer base. With a limited presence in the market, they needed strategies to convey their unique offering and sustainable message to a larger audience. As part of an educational assignment, our goal was to help Rekoroben fulfill their goals.
We developed a concise yet comprehensive document, outlining tailored marketing strategies, audience insights and media tactics. This informed Rekoroben on effective methods to elevate their presence and engage in their target market.
We crafted a captivating quote, “Reuse like there’s a tomorrow”, reflecting Rekoroben’s commitment to sustainability. Designed in their signature font and colors, the quote adorned tote bags, blending aesthetics with eco-awareness, serving as mobile billboard for the brand.
Last but not least we planned an engaging event centered around sustainability. Combining champagne with a sustainability quiz, the participants could learn during a pleasant experience while Rekoroben promoted their ethos.
